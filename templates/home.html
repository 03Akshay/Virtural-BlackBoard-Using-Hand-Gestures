Virtual BlackBoard

Turn Learning into a fun Adventure. Write and doodle on Screen with just your fingertips. Let's start the begin.

Live Drawing Python Code Execution

To draw on the board,
Bring your index and thumb fingers close together without any gap, then move them to create lines.
To erase the board, press 'Ctrl + Z'.
If you want to hide the board temporarily, press the 'Esc' key.
Have fun expressing your creativity!
